Assumptions & Interpretation

  • The calculation corresponds to a standard European call.
  • The payoff is considered only at the moment of maturity (exercise is not possible before).
  • The risk-free rate is continuously compounded and constant over the option lifetime.
  • The input volatility is annualized and represents the constant standard deviation of log returns.
  • The result is a theoretical model value under Black-Scholes-Merton assumptions, not a market guarantee or price quote.
